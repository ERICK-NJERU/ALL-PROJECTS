CYBER CAFE'

Enjoy our cyber café offering reliable high-speed internet, comfortable workstations, and a relaxed, modern atmosphere—an ideal, cool place to work online. Fast Wi-Fi, plenty of power outlets, quiet zones for focused work, and friendly staff ensure uninterrupted productivity for students, freelancers, and remote teams.

Design systems & Software

Design systems and software deliver cohesive product experiences: reusable UI components, accessibility standards, design tokens, prototypes, APIs, and scalable front‑end/back‑end architectures. We build component libraries, production code and deployment pipelines to ensure consistent UX and faster delivery. Securely.

Device Repair & Maintenance

Comprehensive device repair and maintenance for PCs, printers, and mobile devices: hardware diagnostics, component replacement, software troubleshooting, OS repair, virus/malware removal, data backup/recovery, driver & firmware updates, network fixes, printer servicing, and preventive maintenance to maximize uptime, with prompt turnaround.

Disneyland Daycare

Modern Disneyland Daycare — a safe, inspiring space combining creative play, technology‑enhanced learning, and nurturing care. Certified staff deliver age‑appropriate STEM, arts, and outdoor programs, healthy meals, and flexible hours to support families while fostering curiosity, social skills, and confidence. Onsite care.

IT courses Coaching & Mentoring

IT Courses & Coaching — Practical, hands-on training in computer fundamentals, office suites, web and software development, UI/UX design, networking, and cybersecurity. Flexible group or one-to-one tuition, exam prep, mentoring, and career guidance to build skills and confidence. Career guidance certificates, job aid.

Modern Library Acess

Quiet study zones, reservable group rooms, extensive digital collections (e-books, journals, databases) and multimedia resources. Professional reference support includes research consultations, literature searches, citation guidance, and tailored help for students, faculty and researchers. Open-access terminals, printing.

Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Here are the most commonly askes questions.

What services does Seriana IC provide?

We offer a full package of ICT solutions and services including cyber café access, branding and design, device repair, IT courses, student coaching, library access, and a daycare service (Disneyland).

Where is Seriana IC located?

We are based in Nkubu-Meru, Kenya, serving both local clients and online customers across the region.

Do you offer computer training and courses?

Yes. We provide practical, hands-on training in computer packages, web and software development, networking, cybersecurity, and digital skills.

Can I get help with repairing my phone, laptop, or printer?

Yes. Our device repair and maintenance team handles phones, laptops, desktops, and printers quickly and reliably.

Does Seriana IC support students and parents?

Yes. We provide tuition and coaching for students, as well as daycare services to support parents while they work or study.
